About Me

I am an aspiring digital media artist. I plan on being able to work in motion graphics. I enjoy listening to music ranging from EDM to Jazz Hip-hop Instrumental. I pretty much only like sushi and junk food. I try to make my work feel as dreamlike as possible and take a lot of inspiration from the night sky. This is partially due to my day to day experience of being able to recall dreams often and spacing out during the day.
